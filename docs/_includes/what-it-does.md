Yamete sits in your menu bar and watches the built-in accelerometer, the microphone, and your AirPods. When anything feels like a smack, it reacts: plays a sound, flashes the screen, flickers the keyboard backlight, fires a macOS notification. Every output is independently configurable. Every system event (USB plug, power adapter, Bluetooth connect, display hotplug, sleep/wake) can also trigger reactions — each with its own per-output toggle.
